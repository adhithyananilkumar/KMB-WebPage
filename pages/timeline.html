Website Development Timeline

Chronological history of the BCA 2024–2028 student website project with credits and milestones.

July 2024 – Project Initiation

BCA 2024–2028 students brainstormed and planned the website project.

  • Team Leads: Aadhithyan & Group
  • Phase: Planning

September 2024 – UI/UX Design

Wireframes, layout, color scheme, and typography finalized.

  • Design Team: UI/UX Students
  • Phase: Design

January 2025 – Development Started

Front-end with HTML, CSS, JS; back-end integrated with Flask & SQL demo database.

  • Developers: Front-end & Back-end Students
  • Phase: Development

June 2025 – Testing & Feedback

Bugs fixed, responsive layout refined, feedback incorporated.

  • QA Team: Testing Students
  • Phase: Testing

August 2025 – Website Launch

Official release of BCA 2024–2028 student project website. Credits added for all contributors.

  • Credits: BCA 2024–2028 Students & Faculty Mentors
  • Phase: Launch
