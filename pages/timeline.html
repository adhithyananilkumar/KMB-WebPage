Our Mission Journey

A visual timeline of our commitment, showing nearly two decades of growth in care across rural Kerala.

2005

Humble Founding

The KMB mission began with dedicated missionaries establishing a single, simple rural building, caring for the first five elderly individuals.

2008

Rural Health Outreach

Established our Health Outreach to combat local medical gaps, focusing on mobile checkups and chronic care management in surrounding villages.

2014

Mental Rehab Wing

A specialized, secure wing was opened to provide dignified, compassionate care for the abandoned mentally ill, promoting stability.

2020

Sustainable Nutrition

Achieved 100% sustainable nutrition by integrating local farming and community sourcing, ensuring reliable, high-quality meals daily for residents.

2025

Central Hub Goal

Ongoing mission to secure funding to unite our small, scattered setups into a centralized, modern medical-missionary hub for scaled services.
