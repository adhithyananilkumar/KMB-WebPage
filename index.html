Our Core Health Initiatives

We focus on holistic recovery in an environment built on dignity and respect, specializing in areas often overlooked in rural care.

Primary & Chronic Care

Free medical check-ups and managed care for chronic illnesses (diabetes, BP) for all residents and local villagers.

Mental Health Rehabilitation

Specialized long-term support and non-pharmacological therapy for the mentally ill abandoned in our care.

Geriatric & Nutritional Aid

Focusing on the dietary needs of the elderly and frail, ensuring sustainable and locally-sourced nutrition daily.

Our Rural Network: Reaching the Unreached

Our mission operates through a chain of **small, decentralized, non-profit setups** across remote villages in Kerala. This approach ensures deeply localized care but requires centralized management and transparent funding.

Deep Local Presence (8 Centers)

Operating where infrastructure is minimal, ensuring no one is forgotten in their local area.

Unifying Unorganized Setups

Standardizing care, training, and accountability across all centers for maximum efficiency.

Volunteer & Missionary Driven

Care provided by dedicated, full-time missionary teams and local volunteers, fulfilling our Christian mandate.
