Who We Are

Kunjachan Missionary Bhavan (KMB) is a missionary-led home offering shelter, nutrition, therapy, learning support and companionship for the poor, abandoned and elderly.

Our Mission

To serve with love and integrity—ensuring each person is safe, respected and supported to live with dignity.

Our Vision

Communities transformed by compassion where no one is left behind and hope is renewed daily.
